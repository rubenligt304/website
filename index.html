Intro

Welcome my name is Ruben, I am a professional racing driver for Redeker Motorsports, I race in the RMC(Rotax Max Challenge) euro trophy,I myself drive in the rotax max senior class which is the most driven class in the RMC. By the way, check out my awesome journey.

I am a highly talented racing driver with the dream to make it my work and compete against the best racing drivers of the world and compete against the most admired teams of all time, for example: porsche, ferrari, mercedes. altough racing is one of the most demanding sports I'll give it my all to achieve it.

Karting

Karting is a total different sport compared to others, some people might even think its no sport at all.

let me convince you that it actually is, lets start with fitness, you actually need to be very fit because you have to focus 100% of the race and concentration is more demanding than you might think. furthermore your body needs to be in top condition because you take every corner with atleast 3g-forces and when the track has good grip so you'll be able to take it with more speed which leads to more pressure on yourself.

Journey

I started out karting when i was 13 years of age and used to go indoor rental karting when we decided to let me join a karting acedemy which was still indoor, about a half year through i started competing in clubraces with friends I made in the acedemy.

In may 2023 I did a test run in a rotax senior kart which i absolutely fell in love with, the speed, the weight, the g-forces its really all increadible so i joined a racing team called redeker motorsports.

where I now am on a great path to one of the best racing drivers of Europe.
